Our story


Pizzetta is all about quality you can trust. Our sole mission is making the freshest, tastiest and funnest Pizza around. Our classic pan pizza will always be a fan favourite, with a soft and chewy crust perfectly balancing out the healthy tomato puree and mozzarella - cheddar blended cheese. Our authentic Italian crust for those who would prefer a light and airy crust to more fully enjoy the toppings. Thin, light and delicious.

Our newest addition of Puree sauces will blow your mind. Chose between a spicy buffalo, sweet bbq, tangy chipotle can creamy makhni to perfectly compliment your toppings and crust. Our suggestions of combinations might be helpful but ultimately the power lies with you.
